Features

Business Problem:

Airlines struggle to understand why customer satisfaction is declining and which service areas (e.g., check-in, cleanliness, food, seat comfort) are impacting overall experience. Fragmented data makes it difficult for stakeholders to identify dissatisfied customer segments and operational bottlenecks.

Goal of the Dashboard:

The goal of the dashboard is to provide a consolidated, data-driven view of passenger satisfaction levels by analyzing patterns across age groups, travel types, and cabin classes. It highlights key service areas that drive both positive and negative customer experiences, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions that enhance overall customer satisfaction and improve operational performance.

Walkthrough of Key Visuals:

- Key KPIs Revenue - 55.3 M Total Transaction - 45 M Total Interest - 7.8 M Income - 576 M Customer Satisfaction - 3.19.
- Interactive slicers lets users filter all visuals by selected Gender, Card Type, Income Range, Transaction Type, Quarter and date.
- A detailed table showing Card Category, Revenue, Total transaction and interest earned for that card category. 
- Revenue By Expenditure Type , Education, Customer Job, Card Category, Use Chip (column Chart) Displays the distribution of Revenue by Expenditure Type( ex Bills, Entertainment etc) , Education level(Graduate, High School etc),Customer Job (Businessman, SelfEmployee etc), Card Category(Silver, Platinum etc), Use Chip(Swipe, Online,Swipe).
- QTR Revenue and sum of total transaction count (Combo Charts) Displays sum of revenue and total transactions for each quarter.
- Revenue By Week (Line Chart) Displays weekly revenue split by male and female customers.
- A tabular view containing customer job type, salary details, interest earned, and the total salary amount.
- Revenue by Age Group, Income, Dependent Count, Top 5 States, Marital Status, Education level (Stacked Column Chart) Stacked Column chart showing: Revenue insights categorized by multiple attributes and split by gender.
  
Business Impact :

The dashboard helps improve customer experience by identifying low-rated service areas and enabling targeted enhancements across age groups and travel classes. It supports operational efficiency through delay analysis and drives segmentation-based decisions for different customer types. These insights also boost revenue potential through better retention strategies and personalized services, while enabling strategic planning by highlighting key pain points that require focused investments.
